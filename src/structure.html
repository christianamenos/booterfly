Grid

Grids are usefull to structure your content over the page defining zones or columns of diferent sizes. The grid is 12-column based to cover the major scenarios.

Fixed grid

This grid works responsiveness maintaining the percentage of the columns independent of the device viewport size.

  • Use push-X to push X columns the next column to the right. Where push-X is push-1 to push-12.

Bold text

Stand out words within a sentence and make them important. It will also improve your SEO if you are able to mark your keywords. Also available as strong class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><strong>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</strong></p>

Italic text

Emphatize certains words within a sentence and make them important. It will also improve your SEO if you are able to mark your keywords. Also available as italic class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><em>Loremipsum dolor sit amet.</em></p>

Strike text

It is useful when you want to mark a wrong sentence and you want users note it. Also available as strike class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><del>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</del></p>

Underline text

It is useful when you want to mark a wrong sentence that you wrote into the past and is no longer available but you want your user to note it. Also available as underline class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><ins>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</ins></p>

Mark text

It is useful when you want to highlight a part of a text. Also available as highligth class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><mark>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</mark></p>

Small text

As it name says redice the size of the text and mark it as less important taking care of SEO. Also available as small class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p><small>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</small></p>

Sup text

Raise and reduce letters. Very useful if you want to express formulas. Also available as sup class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p>Lorem <sup>ipsum</sup> dolor sit amet.</p>

Sub text

Fall and reduce letters. Very useful if you want to express formulas. Also available as sub class.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

<p>Lorem <sub>ipsum</sub> dolor sit amet.</p>
